Our Family

The RK Ranch was founded in 2020 by the Spilman family after Kelli's family farm sold to a subdivision investing group earlier that year. Kelli's dad Raymond Franz was an advid and well known rice and cattle farmer from Katy, Texas. This is where the name RK came from.

After passing away from a long battle with Parkinsons Disease in 2013, Raymond left his cattle behind. The Spilman family along with Raymond's other children decided to keep his legacy alive. Through lots of trials and tribulations, his cattle is still thriving in all different parts of Texas.

Kelli, her husband Mark, daughter Lyndsi and her husband Josh, and youngest daughter Laci, all take care of the RK. They are happy to answer any questions you may have and love sharing their passion of farming and ranching with everyone.

Our Ranch

At the RK, we are committed to producing quality cattle for the registered seedstock producer and quality replacements for the commercial producer. There are some things you don't have to know exactly how it works. The main thing is that it works. While some people are studying the roots, others are picking the fruit. It just depends which end of this you want to get in on.

We have put an emphasis on structure, growth and maternal traits in our females. TDR is home to 3 out of the top 10 herd sires in the entire Brangus breed: John Wayne 44L, New Direction 000M24 and Tank 165N3. These outstanding Herd Sires are producing efficient, high-volume cattle.
